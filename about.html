About Us

We want you to discover the fascinating world of cars — their past, present, and future. Our mission is to share everything that makes the automotive world so exciting: its rich history, the evolution of design and technology, and the amazing innovations shaping the roads of tomorrow.

Here, you can explore stories about legendary models, learn where and how cars are sold across different countries, and dive into the latest trends that are changing the way we drive. Whether you’re a lifelong car enthusiast or just starting to get curious, we’re here to make your journey fun, informative, and inspiring.

We believe that cars are more than just machines — they connect people, spark emotions, and tell stories about progress and passion. So grab a seat, stay curious, and join us as we explore the ever-evolving world of automobiles together.

Valerie Mokanu, student of DSLIT — creator of CarWorld
